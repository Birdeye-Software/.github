## About Birdeye
Birdeye is a comprehensive reputation management platform that helps businesses monitor, manage, and improve their online presence across multiple review sites, social media platforms, and business directories. The platform provides tools for tracking customer feedback, responding to reviews, and analyzing reputation metrics to help businesses understand and enhance their customer experience. Available for both MacOS and Windows, Birdeye serves as a centralized command center for online reputation, enabling businesses to maintain positive brand perception, address customer concerns promptly, and leverage positive feedback for marketing and business growth.

The platform's monitoring capabilities span hundreds of review sites and social platforms, providing real-time alerts when new reviews are posted and sentiment analysis to identify trends in customer feedback. Birdeye features automated review generation tools that help businesses proactively collect reviews from satisfied customers through SMS, email, and QR code campaigns. With competitive benchmarking, detailed reporting dashboards, and integration with popular CRM and marketing platforms, the platform provides businesses with actionable insights to improve customer satisfaction, increase review volume, and maintain a strong online reputation that drives customer acquisition and retention.
